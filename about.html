About AskABD Technologies

Transforming Business Challenges Into Digital Solutions

Who We Are

AskABD Technologies is a B2B software development company specializing in enterprise solutions, AI/ML development, and digital transformation services. Based in Hyderabad, India, we work with a global network of 50+ expert developers to deliver cutting-edge technology solutions.

Our Mission

We exist to transform complex business challenges into elegant digital solutions. Our mission is to empower businesses with technology that drives growth, efficiency, and innovation.

Our Approach

We believe in:

  • Precision: Every line of code, every feature, every design decision is intentional.
  • Transparency: Clear documentation, regular updates, and honest communication throughout the project lifecycle.
  • Excellence: We don't just build software—we build solutions that scale, perform, and deliver ROI.
  • Partnership: We work as an extension of your team, invested in your success.

Our Team

Our distributed team model allows us to tap into global talent while maintaining agile collaboration. With expertise spanning:

  • Mobile development (iOS, Android, React Native, Flutter)
  • AI/ML engineering (TensorFlow, PyTorch, NLP)
  • Cloud architecture (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • DevOps & infrastructure automation
  • UI/UX design & product strategy
